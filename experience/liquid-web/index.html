// Networking

Designing the Network

I built a carrier-grade network from scratch in a secondary city. I sourced dark fiber IRUs, connected to the local ILEC over a DWDM ring, and extended lit services to upstream providers in larger metros. I ran BGP with full tables for external routing, EIGRP internally, and managed IX Peering, ARIN IP allocations, AS numbers, and DDoS mitigation through Arbor Networks. I owned the entire lifecycle — provider selection, contract negotiation, equipment procurement, Cisco and Arista configuration, fiber termination, and ongoing monitoring with Cacti, SNMP, and Netflow.

// Data Centers

Building Data Centers

I designed three data center facilities from scratch — hand-drew the floor plans, designed the complete power chain from utility feed through rack-level branch circuit monitoring, and led an $80M ground-up build with a dedicated electrical substation. I managed all contractors, utility coordination, and equipment procurement across every build. I also drywalled the first data center, drilled through concrete walls for fiber, and terminated Cat 5e — when you're building from nothing, you do whatever needs doing.

// Cloud Platform

Storm on Demand

I helped build our IaaS platform before "cloud" was mainstream — Xen and KVM hypervisors, self-service provisioning, object storage, load balancing, and firewall configuration. I helped recruit the engineering team, define the product spec, and build the physical and network infrastructure that powered it.

// Linux

Linux at Scale

About 85% of our fleet ran CentOS — thousands of servers with custom Kernel builds, RPM packaging, PXE bare-metal provisioning, Apache, Nginx, and MySQL optimization. We hardened servers, monitored hardware and software, managed security patches, and kept uptime high across the entire fleet.

// The Exit

$225M Exit

I was one of five executives who led the sale to Madison Dearborn Partners in 2015. I prepared most of the data room, built financial models with our accountants, and personally presented to every potential acquirer throughout a multi-month process that culminated in a successful close.
